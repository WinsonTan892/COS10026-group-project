Cybersecurity Analyst

Job Description

The role of a Cybersecurity Analyst within our company generally revolves around safeguarding the information systems and networks of a client's organization from cyber threats. Similarly to the job position: Software Engineer, there are many responsibilities and requirements for this position. An individual with this position can perform their duties on-site or at home. For more details regarding this positon, talk to our Head of Cybersecurity: Justin Hardie.

Responsibilities

  1. Identifying the vulnerabilities in the systems and networks of a client's organisation.
  2. Monitoring systems and networks for potential security breaches or suspicious activities.
  3. Investigating any incidents of security breach, taking preventive measures for such a scenario from reoccuring.
  4. Creating and implement security policies and procedures to ensure that the safety of an organisation's system and network.
  5. Remain up-to-date with the latest cybersecurity threats and security technologies.

Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, or any related field.
  • Strong base knowledge regarding networking and operating systems.
  • Familiarity with cybersecurity principles and technologies such as intrusion detection systems, firewalls, and encryption.
  • Knowledge of IT security standards and frameworks concerning cybersecurity, examples of such include: ISO 27000, NIST SP 800-53, HITRUST Common Security Framework, COBIT, etc.
  • Certifications in Cybersecurity, such as: CISSP, CISA, CISM, CEH, SSCP, etc.

Preferable Traits

  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Excellent communication and teamwork skills.
  • Previous work experience in the Cybersecurity field.

Work Hours

9am - 5pm from Monday to Friday

Salary Range

Annual Salary of $133335 - $166665
