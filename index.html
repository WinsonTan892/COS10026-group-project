Software Engineer

A software engineer is a professional who specializes in the development, design, testing, and maintenance of software applications, systems, and platforms. They use a variety of programming languages, frameworks, and tools to create efficient, reliable, and scalable software solutions that meet the needs of their clients or end-users.
